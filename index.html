Built on AWS Infrastructure

AWS for Startups

AI-native from day one, running entirely on AWS

refair.me is not a product with AI bolted on — it is AI at its core. Every feature depends on AI running on Amazon Bedrock: relationship extraction, connection scoring, warm-path discovery, and real-time opportunity alerts are all inference workloads running continuously across our user base.

Our entire stack lives on AWS. Bedrock powers the intelligence layer. S3 stores the relationship graph data. RDS handles structured user and connection records. Lambda processes event-driven enrichment jobs. CloudFront delivers the platform globally. AWS is not a vendor choice — it is our infrastructure.

We are a seed-stage startup scaling fast. Our AI inference volume grows with every new user onboarded, and our AWS footprint scales in lockstep. We are the kind of AI-native company AWS for Startups was designed to support.
